About SSEIC

Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant is a reputable and well-established company in the field of study abroad and Other immigration services.

22+ Years of experience in visa and immigrations services

over +22 years of experience, the company has built a strong foundation and gained extensive expertise in assisting individuals in pursuing their educational and immigration goals.








Founded by Mr. Sanjeev Kumar, a visionary and experienced entrepreneur, Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant has been at the forefront of guiding students and professionals towards their dreams of studying and settling abroad. Under his leadership, the company has flourished and earned a reputation for its commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

The core focus of Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant lies in providing comprehensive and reliable services for study abroad opportunities in countries like Canada, Australia, UK, USA, and Singapore. Whether it's helping students choose the right educational institution, preparing and submitting applications, or providing guidance on visa processes, the company offers end-to-end support to make the journey smooth and successful.

What sets Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant apart is its dedication to building trust and fostering long-term relationships with clients. The company prides itself on being a trusted immigration consultant, ensuring that clients receive accurate and up-to-date information, personalized guidance, and meticulous attention to detail throughout the application and immigration process.



The team at Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant comprises experienced professionals who are well-versed in the intricacies of international education systems and immigration regulations. Their expertise, combined with the company's extensive network of partner institutions and organizations, allows them to offer tailored solutions that align with the unique needs and aspirations of each client.

Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant is driven by a passion for empowering individuals to achieve their educational and career goals. By providing reliable services, transparent processes, and unwavering support, the company aims to make the study abroad and immigration journey a rewarding and transformative experience for its clients. With a track record of success and a commitment to excellence, Sure Success Education & Immigration Consultant stands as a trusted partner for anyone seeking guidance and assistance in their pursuit of international education and immigration opportunities.
